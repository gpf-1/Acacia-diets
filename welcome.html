Site aims:

record meal data - count (estimate) the calories


enter new recipes to extend the database
allow amendment of recipes by user


assess meal history for balance of
- proportions of food types,
- proportions of carbs, proteins & fats
- fibre, vitamins and minerals needed
- suggest meals that could correct an unbalanced diet


record exercise (type, duration) - estimate calories burnt
calorie difference: equivalent to loss / gain / same weight


give useful information this site has articles, data, tips, advice and information in both short and detailed formats in easy read format
